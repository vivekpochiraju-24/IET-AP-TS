What Is BioSentinel 360?

BioSentinel 360 is an intelligent mobile robot designed to monitor vital signs like heart rate, temperature, SpO₂, and ECG in real time.

  • Monitors vitals & detects falls
  • Connects to doctors remotely via video call
  • Uses voice and gesture control
  • Open-source and buildable at home

Features

Real-Time Health Monitoring
Holographic Display of Vitals
Voice/Gesture Interaction
Cloud Sync & Emergency Alerts
Mobile App + Bluetooth Pairing

Future Vision

AI for Disease Prediction
Blockchain for Health Records
Integration with Wearables & Drones
Multilingual Rural Interface
